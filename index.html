About Julie



Front-end developer with experience in Ruby on Rails applications. With a passion for beautiful and functional design, I want to create things that are meaningful. As a developer, I enjoy the ability to take an idea and make it a reality. The tech industry is ever-evolving which makes it so fascinating and exciting to continuously learn and grow.

When I'm not coding, I love traveling, exploring and taking pictures of everything and anything - it's my main source of inspiration to fuel creativity. I'm also a huge foodie and a fitness enthusiast. I workout so I can eat delicious food. Striving to live a happy and healthy life, I hope to make a positive impact in the world.

I'm currently open for opportunities in the Seattle area. You can contact me below, or just email me at julieparkdev@gmail.com. I would love to hear from you!
